## The 12-Month Master Thesis

> *"If I invest the next 12 months into BG AI Factory, what specific roadmap would make it the most comprehensive AI-powered software development operating system in the world?"*

**The Roadmap to World Domination:**
1. **Months 1-3 (The Foundation):** Fix the architecture. Migrate to PostgreSQL, implement Redis caching, and build the Docker Sandbox. The AI must be able to write code and *test it in a real container* before showing it to the user.
2. **Months 4-6 (The Enterprise Pivot):** Build the governance layer. RBAC, SAML SSO, and Multi-tenant workspaces. This allows you to sell the platform to $50M/yr Software Agencies instead of $10/mo indie developers.
3. **Months 7-9 (The Ecosystem):** Launch the "Agent Marketplace". Allow users to build, share, and monetize custom specialized agents (e.g., an "iOS Swift Expert Agent" or a "Smart Contract Auditor Agent").
4. **Months 10-12 (The OS):** Release BG AI Factory v2. Integrate HR, Billing, and Marketing agents natively into the PIKB. At this stage, a founder does not just build software—they run their entire company from the BG AI Factory CEO Dashboard.

**Conclusion:** 
BG AI Factory is currently the most philosophically and architecturally ambitious project in the AI dev-tool space. By executing this 12-month roadmap, it will transcend from being an "AI Coder" to becoming the **Operating System of the AI Economy.**
